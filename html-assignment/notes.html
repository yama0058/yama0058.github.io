5 HTML tags I have learnt

Ordered List "OL"
It organizes a list
Head "head"
The child tag, Brain. Were information about the documents and links (the CSS documents) is. Information in which are not displayed for users.
Quote "q"
A small quotation embedded within other content. e.g “You become what you think.”
Emphasis "em"
A string of emphasized, slightly more important text. Screen readers will change their voice for this text
Line Break "br"
creates a line break that's significant to the content. Useful in poems and addresses where the division of lines is important.
